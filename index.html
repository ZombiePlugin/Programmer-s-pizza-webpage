THE PROGRAMMER'S PIZZA

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 cup olives, sliced
  • 1/2 cup spinach, optional
  • Olive oil, to brush
  • 1/2 tsp Oregano
  • 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Preheat your oven to its highest temperature. You'll want to slice your vegetables thin so they cook properly. Stretch out your pizza dough on a floured surface. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Brush the dough lightly with olive oil, then spread the pizza sauce evenly, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust. Sprinkle half of your Mozzarella cheese over the sauce. Add your toppings uniformly across the pizza. Top with the remaining Mozzarella, and seasoning with the oregano, crushed red pepper, garlic powder, and a little bit of salt and pepper. Bake your pizza for about 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and starts to brown slightly. Once it's done, remove from the oven and allow it to cool for a few minutes. This will make it easier to cut into slices.

Finally, slice it up and serve hot. Enjoy this programmer's delight - a homemade, fully customizable pizza perfect for working through complex coding challenges or for a gaming night. Eating pizza and solving problems is the ultimate tech tradition!
